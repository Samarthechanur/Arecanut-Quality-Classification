About Us

The project aims to develop a machine learning-based system that can classify and grade arecanut automatically based on its size, colour, texture, and taste. The proposed system will use image processing techniques to extract features from the arecanut images and then use machine learning algorithmsto classify and grade them. The system will be trained using a dataset of arecanut images that have already been classified and graded by experts. The proposed system has several advantages over the traditional manual method of arecanut classification and grading, including accuracy, efficiency, and the elimination of human errors and biases. This system will be a significant contribution to the arecanut industry by enabling it to maintain consistent quality,resulting in increased customer satisfaction and higher profits.
